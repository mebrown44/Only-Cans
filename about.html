Our Mission

At OnlyCans, our mission is to keep our streets clean by making waste management efficient. We track the fullness of public trash cans with community reports and alert waste management companies when bins need attention.

How We Work

Users can report the fullness of trash cans via our website. When two or more users report a trash can as being nearly full (above 4.5 on a scale of 1-5), a notification is sent to the respective waste management company. This ensures timely cleaning and reduces overflowing waste.

Join Us

Want to make your city cleaner? Join our community by reporting full trash cans and helping keep your neighborhood clean. Sign up for notifications and be part of the solution!
